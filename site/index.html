The BioSense method

Four shifts that change everything.

BioSense isn't another AI chatbot. It's a system that understands your body — and gets sharper the longer you use it.

01 — Unify

Every signal, one timeline.

Wearables, blood results, family history, self-reported inputs — all continuously stitched into a single, coherent picture of you.

02 — Learn

Trained on you, not averages.

Your baseline, your trends, your context. BioSense learns what normal looks like for your body, so deviation actually means something.

03 — Guide

Real-time, right-sized guidance.

The right insight at the right time. Quiet when all is well, precise when something matters. Never noisy, never generic.

04 — Compound

Small inputs, long lifespan.

BioSense turns continuous awareness into compounding outcomes — feeling better this week, performing stronger this year, living longer.

Frequently asked

Answers, before you ask.

BioSense is a preventative health intelligence platform. It brings together your wearables, blood biomarkers, family history, and self-reported data into one continuously-learning system — and translates it into clear, real-time guidance designed entirely around you.
BioSense isn't a conversation looking for a query. It's a system that understands your baseline, your context, and your goals, and proactively surfaces the right insight at the right time — without you having to ask the right question.
At launch: Apple Watch, Oura, Whoop, Garmin, and major blood-panel providers. Plus structured inputs for family history, genetic tests, sleep studies, and self-reports. Clinical-record integrations follow.
Your data is yours. It's encrypted in transit and at rest, never sold, and never used to train models on anyone else's behalf. You can export or delete it at any time.
BioSense provides insight and guidance to help you understand your biology. It is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Where clinical care is needed, BioSense helps you and your clinician have a better-informed conversation.
