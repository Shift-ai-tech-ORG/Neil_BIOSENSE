01 — Unification

One timeline, not ten apps.

Wearables, bloods, genetics, history, self-reports — they all sit on different axes and different time scales. BioSense aligns them onto a single continuous timeline, so a 3 a.m. sleep dip connects to tomorrow's training load, which connects to a trending biomarker, which connects to a family history flag.

The result is a view of your biology that no single device — and no single clinician — can give you on its own.

02 — Context

A number only matters next to your baseline.

"Normal" isn't a population average. It's what's normal for you. BioSense builds a personal baseline across every signal, then measures meaning against your trend — not someone else's.

Context is also temporal. A resting heart rate of 62 is unremarkable on its own, and informative if it's 12 points above your own baseline on the same day your sleep score dropped and inflammation ticked up.

03 — Trust & privacy

Your data is yours. Full stop.

BioSense is built on the principle that your biology should belong to you. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. It is never sold, never brokered, and never used to train models on anyone else's behalf.

You can export, port, or delete your data at any time. Access controls are transparent and revocable. If a clinician needs a picture, you decide what they see.
